About Brew & Beans

A neighborhood café rooted in community, sustainable sourcing, and roasted-to-order coffee.

Our Story

We started as a small roasting operation with a big goal: to bring people together through great coffee. Our beans are sourced from ethical farms, roasted in small batches, and shared with a community that values warmth and conversation.
